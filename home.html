Using the free KochZap App and your smartphone, you can easily check to determine if a product is from a Koch Industries company. With a Thumbs-up you know you know your money won't be helping to fund the Koch environmental exploitation machine.

Launch the KochZap app, scan the price tag and you are done. If the item that you are considering shows up as being a Koch product you will be given the red Thumbs-down response.

The KochZap app works by matching the company prefix included on any product barcode against the companies which are owned by Koch Industries. You can also use the Brand-Logos feature to help recognize items produced using brands controlled by the Koch cartel, which may be being sold by another company.

Currently the KochZap app is only available for Android devices, we hope to support iOS products in the future.
